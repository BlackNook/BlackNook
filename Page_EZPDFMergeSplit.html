EZPDFMergeSplit

EZPDFMergeSplit is a PDF split merge software.

Can help you split and merge PDF specified pages.

Function is as follows

. Support multiple PDF loading reorganizations.

. Manually drag and drop the PDF files.

. A blank page can be inserted.

. Support for multiple page dragging. (Shift+Drag)

. Support for duplicate page copies. (Control+Drag)

. The output splits the merged PDF file.
