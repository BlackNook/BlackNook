ImageMatting

ImageMatting is a image backgound removal software

Can help you remove unwanted backgrounds from your images.

Features:

. Support rectangular processing

. Support mask processing: Use red and green maskings

. Support Alpha-Matting processing: transparent area processing

. Instant preview effect: including contrast mode (single page) / paging mode (double page)

. Support for output PNG files (PNG images with transparent background).

. Support background image settings and mixed output.
